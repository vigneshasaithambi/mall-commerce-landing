Frequently asked questions

What is Fynd Mall Commerce?

It is an omnichannel commerce platform for shopping malls. Every tenant gets a storefront on your domain. Customers shop online and pick up at the mall, or visit the mall and keep buying from home. One cart across every tenant, mall-wide loyalty, same-day delivery, and AI that connects the online and offline experience into one loop.

How is this different from giving each tenant their own website?

When each tenant runs their own site, there is no unified experience. Customers cannot buy from two stores in one go. There is no shared loyalty. And you as the mall operator have zero visibility into what is happening across brands. Mall Commerce solves all of that while still letting each tenant control their own catalogue, pricing, and content.

What AI capabilities are included?

Tenants can build storefront pages by typing a description. AI reads data from every tenant and gives you insights in plain English. Product recommendations work across the entire mall, not just one store. And every tenant's products automatically become visible to AI search engines and assistants without any setup.

Do tenants lose control of their stores?

No. Every tenant controls their own catalogue, pricing, promotions, and storefront design. You as the mall operator manage the shared infrastructure: the domain, the unified checkout, the loyalty programme, and the analytics. It works the same way as a physical mall. Tenants control their stores. You control the building.

Can customers buy from multiple tenants in one transaction?

Yes. Customers add products from any store in the mall to a single cart and pay once. They get one invoice and one place to view all their orders, returns, and refunds.

How does the loyalty programme work?

Customers earn points at any tenant and redeem them at any other tenant in the mall. Points also work for movies, spa, gaming, and events. You set the earn and burn rules, the tiers, and the wallet configuration. The loyalty engine is already used by leading beauty and fashion brands in India.

Can customers get same-day delivery?

Yes. Customers can shop from home and receive products from the mall the same day. They can also buy online and pick up in store at their convenience. The delivery infrastructure is shared across all tenants, so even the smallest store in the mall gets the same delivery speed as the biggest.

How does the digital mall drive footfall to the physical mall?

Every online action is designed to bring customers through the door. They buy online and pick up at the mall. They earn coupons online that can only be redeemed in store. They use loyalty points for movies, spa, and events. They book parking before arriving. The digital mall does not replace the physical mall. It fills it.
